FAQs

How do I schedule a tutoring session with Crunch Tutoring?

Scheduling a tutoring session with Crunch Tutoring is easy! Simply visit our website and fill out the contact form, or give us a call. Our friendly staff will assist you in finding the perfect time and tutor to fit your schedule and academic needs.

Are your tutoring services available online or in person?

We offer both online and in-person tutoring services to accommodate your preferences and needs. Our online platform provides the same personalized attention and quality instruction as our in-person sessions, ensuring flexibility and accessibility for all students.

What subjects do you offer tutoring for?

At Crunch Tutoring, we offer tutoring services for English and Mathematics specifically. However, whether you need help with a specific topic or want comprehensive support across multiple subjects, our experienced tutors are here to assist you.

How are your tutors selected?

Our tutors undergo a rigorous selection process to ensure they meet our high standards of expertise, professionalism, and dedication to student success. We carefully review their qualifications, experience, and teaching style to match them with students who will benefit most from their expertise.
